Martin O'Malley - Democrat

  • Born January 18, 1963 in Washington, D.C.
  • Mayor of Baltimore, MD, 1999-2007
  • Governor of Maryland, 2007-2015
  • Wants college option for undocumented immigrants
  • Wants to legalize same-sex marriage
  • Opposes Keystone Pipeline project
  • Supports Affordable Care Act as a right
  • Supports gun control

Ted Cruz - Republican

  • Born December 22, 1970 in Calgary, Alberta
  • Solicitor General (TX), 2003-2008
  • U.S. Senator (TX), 2013-present
  • Against amnesty for illegal immigrants
  • Believes same-sex marriage should be decided by states
  • Supports approval of Keystone Pipeline
  • Strong critic of Affordable Care Act
  • Supports gun rights under Second Amendment

Jim Webb - Democrat

  • Born February 9, 1946 in St. Louis, MO
  • Assistant Secretary of Defense (Reserve Affairs), 1984-1987
  • U.S. Secretary of the Navy, 1987-1988
  • U.S. Senator (VA), 2007-2013
  • Wants citizenship path for illegal immigrants
  • Supports same-sex marriage
  • Voted in favor of Keystone Pipeline
  • Supports Affordable Care Act
  • Has voted against gun control

Marco Rubio - Republican

  • Born May 28, 1971 in Miami, Florida
  • Florida House of Representatives, 2000-2009
  • U.S. Senator (FL), 2011-present
  • Opposes amnesty for illegal immigrants
  • Against same-sex marriage
  • Wants approval of Keystone Pipeline
  • Opposes Affordable Care Act
  • Supports gun rights

Hillary Clinton - Democrat

  • Born October 26, 1947 in Chicago, Illinois
  • U.S. Senator (NY), 2001-2009
  • Secretary of State, 2009-2013
  • Supports amnesty for illegal immigrants
  • Opposed same-sex marriage last year but now supports it
  • Refuses to share opinion on Keystone Pipeline
  • Supports Affordable Care Act
  • Supports gun control

Rand Paul - Republican

  • Born January 7, 1963 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • U.S. Senator (KY), 2011-present
  • Supports path to citizenship for illegal immigrants
  • Supports same-sex contracts but not marriage
  • Supports approval of Keystone Pipeline
  • Opposes Affordable Care Act
  • Supports gun rights under Second Amendment
